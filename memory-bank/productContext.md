# Product Context
## Why This Project Exists

---

## Problem Statement

Parents seeking mental health services for their children face significant barriers:

1. **Uncertainty About Fit:** Parents don't know if services are appropriate for their child's needs
2. **Complex Onboarding:** Lengthy forms, insurance verification, and scheduling create friction
3. **Emotional Stress:** Seeking help is difficult; parents need support and reassurance
4. **Information Overload:** Insurance details, costs, and clinician options are overwhelming

---

## Solution Overview

**NurtureAI** provides an empathetic, AI-powered onboarding experience that:

- **Assesses Appropriateness:** Conversational AI helps parents understand if Daybreak Health services fit their child's needs
- **Streamlines Process:** Multi-step forms with progress tracking, auto-save, and smart defaults
- **Provides Support:** Empathetic AI tone, clear explanations, and alternative resources when needed
- **Simplifies Decisions:** Insurance matching, cost estimation, and clinician availability all in one place

---

## Target Users

### Primary User: Parents of Children Needing Mental Health Services

**User Characteristics:**
- Concerned about their child's mental health
- May be anxious or uncertain about seeking help
- Need clear, supportive guidance
- Value efficiency and transparency
- Want to understand costs upfront

**User Goals:**
1. Quickly assess if services are appropriate
2. Complete onboarding without frustration
3. Understand insurance coverage and costs
4. Schedule appointments easily
5. Feel supported throughout the process

---

## User Stories

### User Story 1: Mental Health Assessment
**As a parent,** I want to have a conversational assessment with an AI assistant about my child's mental health concerns, **so that** I can understand if Daybreak Health services are appropriate for my child's needs.

**Key Features:**
- Friendly, non-judgmental conversation
- Adaptive questions based on responses
- Covers mood, behavior, social interactions, academic performance
- Clear feedback on service suitability
- 10-15 minute completion time

### User Story 2: Insurance Information Submission
**As a parent,** I want to easily submit my insurance information through a simple form or image upload, **so that** I can quickly move forward without confusion or frustration.

**Key Features:**
- Manual entry OR insurance card upload
- Image-to-text extraction (OCR)
- Clear validation and progress indicators
- Save and return later capability

### User Story 3: Emotional Support & Reassurance
**As a parent,** I want to receive supportive messages and guidance throughout the process, **so that** I feel confident and less anxious about seeking help for my child.

**Key Features:**
- Empathetic AI tone throughout
- Reassuring messages at key transitions
- Clear explanation of next steps
- Option to connect with human support
- Acknowledgment of difficulty in seeking help

---

## Product Principles

1. **Empathy First:** Every interaction should be supportive and non-judgmental
2. **Transparency:** Clear about costs, coverage, and what to expect
3. **Efficiency:** Minimize time and friction in onboarding
4. **Safety:** Crisis detection and appropriate resource recommendations
5. **Honesty:** If Daybreak isn't a fit, recommend alternatives clearly

---

## Key Differentiators

1. **AI-Powered Assessment:** Not just a form - a conversational experience that adapts
2. **RAG Enhancement:** Evidence-based responses using validated mental health screening tools
3. **Real Data Integration:** Uses actual clinician availability and insurance data for accurate matching
4. **Cost Transparency:** Upfront cost estimation based on real coverage data
5. **Supportive Experience:** Designed to reduce anxiety, not increase it

---

## Success Indicators

- **Completion Rate:** High percentage of users completing full onboarding
- **Time to Complete:** Average onboarding time <15 minutes
- **User Satisfaction:** Positive feedback on empathetic AI and clear process
- **Crisis Detection:** Appropriate flagging and resource recommendations
- **Data Quality:** Accurate insurance matching and cost estimates
